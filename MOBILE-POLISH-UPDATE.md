# V8 Race Fantasy mobile polish update

Changes in this version:

- Pick Team page now clearly tells users to tap/click a driver card to add it to their roster.
- Pick Team completeness checklist has moved to the bottom of the team-picking section near the Save Team button.
- Selected drivers show an "Added" badge on their card.
- My Team page is more compact on mobile:
  - smaller summary stat cards
  - smaller profile/header card
  - horizontal event selector
  - compact saved-team/result rows instead of large boxes
- Removed popular pick stats from the Leaderboard page.
- Added current round fantasy ownership stats to the homepage instead:
  - most picked drivers
  - captain count
  - vice-captain count
  - ownership percentage
  - captain favourite
